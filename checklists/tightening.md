# Tightening Checklist

- Dead code, dead notes, and stale TODOs are removed.
- Naming is consistent and specific.
- Repeated logic is either justified or reduced.
- Hidden assumptions are moved into docs, tests, or code comments.
- Seams are cleaner than they were at the start.
- The handoff state is current.
- The status can be compacted into one clear paragraph.
- Critical tests have been rerun after cleanup.
- The project can stop here without creating confusion.
- If more work remains, the next slice is obvious.
